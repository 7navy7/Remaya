Infant Care & In-Flight Playroom

Travel with peace of mind knowing your little ones are cared for by our professional staff in our dedicated in-flight play area.

Core Services

Supervised Play Area

  • Certified child care professionals on staff
  • Soft play mats and age-appropriate toys
  • Interactive games and activities
  • Regular sanitization of all surfaces and toys

Rest & Feeding Zone

  • Quiet, dimmable lighting for naps
  • Comfortable rocking chairs for feeding
  • Bottle warmers and sterilizers available
  • Private nursing area with curtains
Premium Privileges

Parent Support Services

  • Complimentary diaper bag with essentials
  • On-demand baby food and formula
  • Emergency baby supplies available
  • Priority boarding for families
  • Extra baggage allowance for baby items

Special Amenities

  • Disposable changing pads and wipes
  • Baby toiletries and skincare products
  • Comfortable baby carriers for use onboard
  • Personalized welcome kit for your child

Important Note: This premium service costs double the regular ticket fare and is available only for select long-haul flights. Reservations must be made at least 48 hours before departure.
